Tips for Effective Pest Control

Here are some specific tips to keep those pesky pests at bay: * Seal all entry points: Pests can enter your home or business through even the smallest cracks and crevices. Seal all holes and gaps around windows, doors, and pipes to prevent pests from entering. * Keep your property clean: Keep your home or business clean and clutter-free to reduce the attractiveness to pests. Regularly clean up crumbs, spills, and trash to prevent pests from feeding and breeding. * Eliminate standing water: Standing water can attract pests, such as mosquitoes and rodents. Eliminate standing water around your property, including pet water dishes and bird baths. * Use natural deterrents: Certain plants, such as basil and mint, can repel pests naturally. Plant these in your garden or indoor pots to keep pests away.
