How to Use

This Student Study Planner Notion template is designed to help students organize their study schedule, track their progress, and achieve their academic goals. To get started, simply import this template into your Notion account and follow the instructions below.

📚 Introduction

This template consists of several sections, each with its own database/table and properties. The main sections include:

* Study Schedule * Task List * Note-taking * Progress Tracking * Goal Setting

📅 Study Schedule

The Study Schedule section is where you'll plan out your daily and weekly study routine. The database/table headers and properties are as follows:

* Date (date property) * Time (text property) * Subject (select property: e.g. Math, Science, English) * Topic (text property) * Duration (number property) * Break (checkbox property)

Example rows:

| Date | Time | Subject | Topic | Duration | Break | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 2024-09-16 | 9:00 AM | Math | Algebra | 2 | ☑️ | | 2024-09-16 | 11:00 AM | Science | Biology | 1.5 | ❌ | | 2024-09-17 | 2:00 PM | English | Literature | 2 | ☑️ |

Usage instructions: Create a new row for each study session, filling in the date, time, subject, topic, duration, and break. Use the formula `=Date+Duration` to calculate the end time of each session.

📝 Task List

The Task List section is where you'll keep track of assignments, projects, and other tasks related to your studies. The database/table headers and properties are as follows:

* Task (text property) * Due Date (date property) * Priority (select property: e.g. High, Medium, Low) * Status (select property: e.g. To-Do, In Progress, Done) * Notes (text property)

Example rows:

| Task | Due Date | Priority | Status | Notes | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | Complete math homework | 2024-09-18 | High | To-Do | Chapter 3, problems 1-10 | | Research science project | 2024-09-25 | Medium | In Progress | Need to find 5 sources | | Write English essay | 2024-10-01 | High | Done | Received A- grade |

Usage instructions: Create a new row for each task, filling in the task, due date, priority, status, and notes. Use the formula `=Due Date-Today()` to calculate the number of days left until the deadline.

📝 Note-taking

The Note-taking section is where you'll store notes from lectures, readings, and other study materials. The database/table headers and properties are as follows:

* Note (text property) * Date (date property) * Subject (select property: e.g. Math, Science, English) * Topic (text property) * Tags (multi-select property: e.g. #lecture, #reading, #summary)

Example rows:

| Note | Date | Subject | Topic | Tags | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | Definition of mitosis | 2024-09-15 | Science | Biology | #lecture, #summary | | Summary of chapter 3 | 2024-09-16 | Math | Algebra | #reading, #summary | | Key terms for literature exam | 2024-09-17 | English | Literature | #lecture, #reading |

Usage instructions: Create a new row for each note, filling in the note, date, subject, topic, and tags. Use the formula `=Subject+Topic` to create a unique identifier for each note.

📊 Progress Tracking

The Progress Tracking section is where you'll monitor your progress over time. The database/table headers and properties are as follows:

* Date (date property) * Subject (select property: e.g. Math, Science, English) * Progress (number property: e.g. 0-100%) * Notes (text property)

Example rows:

| Date | Subject | Progress | Notes | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 2024-09-15 | Math | 80 | Completed homework, need to review for test | | 2024-09-16 | Science | 60 | Struggling with biology, need to ask teacher for help | | 2024-09-17 | English | 90 | Finished essay, need to proofread |

Usage instructions: Create a new row for each progress update, filling in the date, subject, progress, and notes. Use the formula `=Progress/100` to calculate the percentage of progress.

🎯 Goal Setting

The Goal Setting section is where you'll set and track long-term goals. The database/table headers and properties are as follows:

* Goal (text property) * Deadline (date property) * Progress (number property: e.g. 0-100%) * Notes (text property)

Example rows:

| Goal | Deadline | Progress | Notes | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | Get A in math class | 2024-12-15 | 70 | Need to focus on homework and review for tests | | Improve reading comprehension | 2024-11-01 | 50 | Need to practice active reading and take notes | | Complete science project | 2024-10-15 | 80 | Need to finish research and start writing report |

Usage instructions: Create a new row for each goal, filling in the goal, deadline, progress, and notes. Use the formula `=Deadline-Today()` to calculate the number of days left until the deadline.

📝 Additional Tips

* Use the @ symbol to mention and link to other pages or databases in your Notion account. * Use the / symbol to create a new page or database. * Use the [ ] symbol to create a toggle list. * Use the {{ }} symbol to create a template.

By following these instructions and using this template, you'll be able to stay organized, focused, and on track to achieving your academic goals. Happy studying! 📚💪
