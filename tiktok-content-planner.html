TikTok Content Planner

How to Use

This TikTok Content Planner template is designed to help you organize and schedule your TikTok content in advance. It includes sections for planning, creating, and tracking your content, as well as analyzing your performance and engagement. To get started, simply import this template into your Notion account and follow the instructions below.

📅 Content Calendar

The Content Calendar section is where you'll plan and schedule your TikTok content. It includes the following properties: * Date: The date you want to post your content * Content Type: The type of content you're posting (e.g. video, photo, live stream) * Caption: The caption for your post * Hashtags: The hashtags you'll use for your post * Engagement Goal: The engagement goal for your post (e.g. likes, comments, shares) * Status: The status of your post (e.g. drafted, scheduled, posted)

Example rows: | Date | Content Type | Caption | Hashtags | Engagement Goal | Status | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 2024-09-16 | Video | "New dance challenge" | #dance #challenge | 100 likes | Drafted | | 2024-09-17 | Photo | "Behind the scenes" | #behindthescenes #photography | 50 comments | Scheduled | | 2024-09-18 | Live Stream | "Q&A session" | #live #qanda | 200 shares | Posted |

📊 Content Performance

The Content Performance section is where you'll track the performance of your TikTok content. It includes the following properties: * Post ID: The ID of the post * Date: The date the post was made * Content Type: The type of content posted * Engagement: The engagement on the post (e.g. likes, comments, shares) * Reach: The reach of the post * Formula: `Engagement Rate = (Engagement / Reach) * 100`

Example rows: | Post ID | Date | Content Type | Engagement | Reach | Engagement Rate | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 12345 | 2024-09-16 | Video | 120 likes | 1000 | 12% | | 12346 | 2024-09-17 | Photo | 60 comments | 500 | 12% | | 12347 | 2024-09-18 | Live Stream | 250 shares | 2000 | 12.5% |

📝 Content Ideas

The Content Ideas section is where you'll brainstorm and store ideas for your TikTok content. It includes the following properties: * Idea: The idea for your content * Content Type: The type of content the idea is for * Category: The category of the idea (e.g. dance, beauty, gaming) * Status: The status of the idea (e.g. drafted, in progress, completed)

Example rows: | Idea | Content Type | Category | Status | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | "New dance challenge" | Video | Dance | Drafted | | "Product review" | Video | Beauty | In Progress | | "Gaming tutorial" | Video | Gaming | Completed |

📈 Hashtag Research

The Hashtag Research section is where you'll research and store hashtags for your TikTok content. It includes the following properties: * Hashtag: The hashtag you're researching * Popularity: The popularity of the hashtag * Relevance: The relevance of the hashtag to your content * Formula: `Hashtag Score = (Popularity * Relevance) / 100`

Example rows: | Hashtag | Popularity | Relevance | Hashtag Score | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | #dance | 1000000 | 90 | 900 | | #beauty | 500000 | 80 | 400 | | #gaming | 2000000 | 95 | 1900 |

📊 Engagement Tracker

The Engagement Tracker section is where you'll track your engagement on TikTok. It includes the following properties: * Date: The date you're tracking engagement * Engagement: The engagement on your content (e.g. likes, comments, shares) * Reach: The reach of your content * Formula: `Engagement Rate = (Engagement / Reach) * 100`

Example rows: | Date | Engagement | Reach | Engagement Rate | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 2024-09-16 | 150 likes | 1000 | 15% | | 2024-09-17 | 80 comments | 500 | 16% | | 2024-09-18 | 300 shares | 2000 | 15% |

📝 Caption Ideas

The Caption Ideas section is where you'll brainstorm and store ideas for your TikTok captions. It includes the following properties: * Idea: The idea for your caption * Content Type: The type of content the caption is for * Category: The category of the caption (e.g. dance, beauty, gaming) * Status: The status of the caption (e.g. drafted, in progress, completed)

Example rows: | Idea | Content Type | Category | Status | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | "Get ready to dance" | Video | Dance | Drafted | | "New product alert" | Video | Beauty | In Progress | | "Level up your game" | Video | Gaming | Completed |

📊 Analytics

The Analytics section is where you'll track your overall performance on TikTok. It includes the following properties: * Date: The date you're tracking analytics * Followers: The number of followers you have * Engagement: The engagement on your content (e.g. likes, comments, shares) * Reach: The reach of your content * Formula: `Engagement Rate = (Engagement / Reach) * 100`

Example rows: | Date | Followers | Engagement | Reach | Engagement Rate | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 2024-09-16 | 1000 | 150 likes | 1000 | 15% | | 2024-09-17 | 1050 | 80 comments | 500 | 16% | | 2024-09-18 | 1100 | 300 shares | 2000 | 15% |

To get the most out of this template, make sure to regularly update your Content Calendar, track your engagement and reach, and analyze your performance using the Analytics section. You can also use the Formula suggestions to calculate your engagement rate and hashtag score. By using this template, you'll be able to plan, create, and track your TikTok content in a organized and efficient way.
