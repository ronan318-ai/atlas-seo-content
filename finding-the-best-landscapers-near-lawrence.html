Red Flags to Watch Out for

When searching for landscapers near Lawrence, there are several red flags to watch out for. Be wary of companies that require full payment upfront, as this can be a sign of a scam. Also, be cautious of landscapers who don't provide a clear estimate or contract, as this can lead to misunderstandings and unexpected costs. Additionally, watch out for companies with poor online reviews or a history of complaints with the Better Business Bureau.
