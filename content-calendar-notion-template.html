How to Use 📅

This Content Calendar Notion Template is designed to help you plan, organize, and schedule your content across multiple platforms. To get started, simply import this template into your Notion account and follow the instructions below.

Introduction 📄

The Content Calendar Notion Template consists of several sections, each with its own database and properties. The main sections include: * Content Ideas: A database for storing and brainstorming content ideas * Content Calendar: A calendar view for scheduling and organizing content * Content Library: A database for storing and tracking published content * Analytics: A database for tracking engagement and performance metrics

Content Calendar 📆

Database Properties

| Property | Type | Description | | --- | --- | --- | | Title | Text | Title of the content piece | | Date | Date | Scheduled publication date of the content piece | | Category | Select | Category or topic of the content piece (e.g. blog post, social media, video) | | Platform | Select | Platform where the content will be published (e.g. Facebook, Twitter, YouTube) | | Status | Select | Status of the content piece (e.g. pending, published, draft) |

Formula Suggestions

* Use the `=DateAdd(Date, 7, "days")` formula to schedule a follow-up post 7 days after the initial publication date * Use the `=Prop("Category") = "Blog Post"` formula to filter and view only blog post content
