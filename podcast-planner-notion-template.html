How to Use 📚

This Podcast Planner Notion template is designed to help you organize and manage your podcast episodes, guests, and production schedule. To get started, simply import this template into your Notion workspace and begin filling in the sections. Each section is designed to help you streamline your podcast production process, from planning and research to recording and publishing.

Production Schedule 📅

This calendar view will help you visualize your podcast production schedule, including recording, editing, and publishing dates.

To create a formula to calculate the production timeline, you can use the following text formula: `=dateAdd(prop("Release Date"), -7, "days")` to calculate the recording date, and `=dateAdd(prop("Release Date"), -3, "days")` to calculate the editing date.
