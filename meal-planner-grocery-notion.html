Meal Planner & Grocery Notion

How to Use 📚

This Meal Planner & Grocery Notion template is designed to help you plan and organize your meals, create grocery lists, and track your expenses. To get started, simply import this template into your Notion account and follow the instructions below.

Introduction 🍴

This template consists of three main databases: Meals, Grocery Lists, and Expenses. The Meals database is used to store information about each meal, including the name, ingredients, cooking time, and nutritional information. The Grocery Lists database is used to create and manage your grocery lists, and the Expenses database is used to track your expenses.

Meals Database 🍔

The Meals database is used to store information about each meal. The following properties are included: * Name: The name of the meal * Ingredients: A list of ingredients required for the meal * Cooking Time: The time it takes to cook the meal * Nutritional Information: Information about the meal's nutritional content, including calories, protein, fat, and carbohydrates * Tags: A list of tags to categorize the meal (e.g. breakfast, lunch, dinner, vegetarian, etc.) * Recipe: A link to the recipe or a brief description of how to prepare the meal

Example rows: | Name | Ingredients | Cooking Time | Nutritional Information | Tags | Recipe | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | Grilled Chicken | Chicken breast, olive oil, salt, pepper | 30 minutes | 350 calories, 35g protein, 10g fat | dinner, protein | [Link to recipe](https://example.com/recipe) | | Veggie Stir-Fry | Broccoli, bell peppers, onions, olive oil | 20 minutes | 200 calories, 5g protein, 10g fat | lunch, vegetarian | Saute veggies in olive oil and serve over rice | | Breakfast Omelette | Eggs, cheese, bell peppers, onions | 15 minutes | 250 calories, 20g protein, 15g fat | breakfast, protein | Whisk eggs and cook in a pan with cheese and veggies |

Grocery Lists Database 🛍️

The Grocery Lists database is used to create and manage your grocery lists. The following properties are included: * List Name: The name of the grocery list * Items: A list of items to purchase * Store: The store where the items will be purchased * Date: The date the list was created * Total Cost: The total cost of the items on the list (calculated using a formula: `=sum(Items.Quantity * Items.Price)`)

Example rows: | List Name | Items | Store | Date | Total Cost | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | Weekly Groceries | Eggs, milk, bread, chicken breast | Walmart | 2023-02-20 | $50.00 | | Monthly Pantry Staples | Rice, pasta, canned goods, olive oil | Costco | 2023-02-15 | $100.00 | | Special Occasion | Cake, ice cream, flowers | Target | 2023-02-25 | $75.00 |

Expenses Database 📊

The Expenses database is used to track your expenses. The following properties are included: * Date: The date the expense was incurred * Category: The category of the expense (e.g. groceries, dining out, etc.) * Amount: The amount of the expense * Description: A brief description of the expense * Tags: A list of tags to categorize the expense (e.g. food, transportation, etc.)

Example rows: | Date | Category | Amount | Description | Tags | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 2023-02-20 | Groceries | $50.00 | Weekly groceries | food, groceries | | 2023-02-25 | Dining Out | $25.00 | Lunch at restaurant | food, dining out | | 2023-02-15 | Pantry Staples | $100.00 | Monthly pantry staples | food, groceries |

Meal Planning Section 📅

This section is used to plan your meals for the week. You can create a new page for each week and add the meals you want to cook. You can also add a grocery list and expenses tracker to each page.

Example page:

Week of 2023-02-20

* Monday: Grilled Chicken * Tuesday: Veggie Stir-Fry * Wednesday: Breakfast Omelette * Grocery List: [Link to grocery list](https://example.com/grocery-list) * Expenses: [Link to expenses tracker](https://example.com/expenses-tracker)

Tips and Tricks 🤔

* Use the tags property to categorize your meals and expenses, making it easier to filter and search for specific items. * Use the formula `=sum(Items.Quantity * Items.Price)` to calculate the total cost of your grocery lists. * Use the date property to track when you created your grocery lists and expenses, making it easier to see when you need to restock or review your expenses. * Use the description property to add notes or comments to your expenses, making it easier to remember what you purchased or why you incurred a certain expense.

Getting Started 🚀

To get started, simply import this template into your Notion account and begin adding your meals, grocery lists, and expenses. You can customize the template to fit your needs and preferences, and use the formulas and properties to make it easier to track and manage your meals and expenses. Happy planning! 🍴
