How to Use

The Notion Life Planner 2026 is a comprehensive digital planner designed to help you organize and manage your daily life, goals, and tasks. This template is divided into several sections, each with its own unique features and functions. To get started, simply import this template into your Notion account and begin filling out the sections as described below.

📆 Calendar

The Calendar section is where you'll plan out your year, month, and day. This section includes the following properties: - Date (date) - Event (text) - Time (time) - Location (text) - Notes (text)

To use this section, simply create a new page for each day or event you want to plan, and fill out the corresponding properties. You can also use the calendar view to see a visual representation of your upcoming events.

Example rows: | Date | Event | Time | Location | Notes | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 2026-01-01 | New Year's Day | 12:00 PM | Home | Watch football game | | 2026-01-15 | Doctor's Appointment | 2:00 PM | Doctor's Office | Bring insurance cards | | 2026-02-14 | Valentine's Day | 7:00 PM | Restaurant | Make reservation |

📝 Goals

The Goals section is where you'll set and track your long-term objectives. This section includes the following properties: - Goal (text) - Category (select: Personal, Career, Financial, Health) - Deadline (date) - Progress (number) - Notes (text)

To use this section, create a new page for each goal you want to set, and fill out the corresponding properties. You can use the formula `=prop("Progress")/100` to calculate your progress as a percentage.

Example rows: | Goal | Category | Deadline | Progress | Notes | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | Learn a new language | Personal | 2026-12-31 | 20 | Take online course | | Get a promotion | Career | 2026-06-30 | 50 | Network with colleagues | | Save $10,000 | Financial | 2026-12-31 | 30 | Create budget |

📊 Budget

The Budget section is where you'll track your income and expenses. This section includes the following properties: - Date (date) - Category (select: Housing, Transportation, Food, Entertainment) - Income (number) - Expense (number) - Notes (text)

To use this section, create a new page for each transaction you want to track, and fill out the corresponding properties. You can use the formula `=sum(prop("Income"))-sum(prop("Expense"))` to calculate your total balance.

Example rows: | Date | Category | Income | Expense | Notes | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 2026-01-01 | Salary | 5000 | 0 | Payday | | 2026-01-05 | Groceries | 0 | 100 | Buy milk and eggs | | 2026-01-10 | Rent | 0 | 1500 | Pay rent |

📝 Tasks

The Tasks section is where you'll manage your daily and weekly to-do lists. This section includes the following properties: - Task (text) - Due Date (date) - Priority (select: High, Medium, Low) - Completed (checkbox) - Notes (text)

To use this section, create a new page for each task you want to complete, and fill out the corresponding properties. You can use the formula `=if(prop("Completed"), "Done", "Not Done")` to mark tasks as completed.

Example rows: | Task | Due Date | Priority | Completed | Notes | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | Finish project report | 2026-01-15 | High | false | Meet with team | | Buy groceries | 2026-01-10 | Medium | false | Buy milk and eggs | | Exercise | 2026-01-05 | Low | true | Go for a run |

📊 Habits

The Habits section is where you'll track your daily habits and routines. This section includes the following properties: - Habit (text) - Frequency (select: Daily, Weekly, Monthly) - Streak (number) - Notes (text)

To use this section, create a new page for each habit you want to track, and fill out the corresponding properties. You can use the formula `=prop("Streak")` to track your progress.

Example rows: | Habit | Frequency | Streak | Notes | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | Exercise | Daily | 10 | Go for a run | | Meditate | Daily | 5 | Use Headspace app | | Read | Weekly | 2 | Read for 30 minutes |

📝 Reflections

The Reflections section is where you'll reflect on your progress and set new goals. This section includes the following properties: - Date (date) - Reflection (text) - Goals (text) - Notes (text)

To use this section, create a new page for each reflection you want to write, and fill out the corresponding properties. You can use this section to review your progress and set new goals for the upcoming week or month.
