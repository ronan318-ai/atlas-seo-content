Introduction 🌟

Welcome to the Goal Setting & Vision Board Notion template! This template is designed to help you achieve your goals and create a vision board to visualize your desires. The template is divided into several sections, each with its own unique purpose.

Main Database 📊

The main database is where you will store all of your goals and vision board items. The database has the following properties: * Goal: A text property to store the name of your goal * Category: A select property to categorize your goal (e.g. career, relationships, health) * Priority: A number property to prioritize your goals * Status: A select property to track the status of your goal (e.g. not started, in progress, completed) * Vision Board: A relation property to link to the vision board database * Tags: A multi-select property to add tags to your goal

Goal Setting 🎯

This section is where you will set and break down your goals into actionable steps. To use this section, simply create a new page and add the following properties: * Goal: The name of your goal * Why: The reason why you want to achieve this goal * How: The steps you will take to achieve this goal * By When: The deadline for achieving this goal * Progress: A formula to track your progress (e.g. `=prop("Completed Tasks")/prop("Total Tasks")`)

Example rows: | Goal | Why | How | By When | Progress | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | Learn a new language | To improve my career prospects | Take an online course, practice for 30 minutes each day | 6 months | 0% | | Start a new business | To be my own boss | Create a business plan, secure funding, launch the business | 1 year | 0% | | Run a marathon | To improve my physical health | Create a training plan, run 3 times a week, track my progress | 3 months | 0% |

Vision Board 🌈

This section is where you will create a vision board to visualize your desires. To use this section, simply create a new page and add the following properties: * Image: An image property to store an image that represents your desire * Description: A text property to describe your desire * Goal: A relation property to link to the goal database * Tags: A multi-select property to add tags to your vision board item

Example rows: | Image | Description | Goal | Tags | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 🏖️ | A beach vacation | Learn a new language | relaxation, travel | | 📚 | A bestselling book | Start a new business | success, writing | | 🏃‍♀️ | A marathon finisher's medal | Run a marathon | fitness, achievement |

Progress Tracking 📈

This section is where you will track your progress towards your goals. To use this section, simply create a new page and add the following properties: * Goal: A relation property to link to the goal database * Task: A text property to store a task related to your goal * Completed: A checkbox property to mark the task as completed * Due Date: A date property to store the due date for the task

Example rows: | Goal | Task | Completed | Due Date | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | Learn a new language | Complete lesson 1 | ❌ | 2024-02-15 | | Start a new business | Secure funding | ❌ | 2024-03-01 | | Run a marathon | Complete a 5K run | ✔️ | 2024-01-15 |

Reflection and Review 📝

This section is where you will reflect on your progress and review your goals. To use this section, simply create a new page and add the following properties: * Date: A date property to store the date of your reflection * Goal: A relation property to link to the goal database * Progress: A text property to store your progress towards your goal * Challenges: A text property to store any challenges you faced * Next Steps: A text property to store your next steps towards your goal

Example rows: | Date | Goal | Progress | Challenges | Next Steps | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | 2024-02-01 | Learn a new language | 20% | Finding time to practice | Create a schedule to practice every day | | 2024-03-01 | Start a new business | 50% | Securing funding | Reach out to investors and apply for loans | | 2024-01-15 | Run a marathon | 30% | Injuries | Create a rehabilitation plan and adjust my training schedule |

Formula Suggestions 🤔

Here are some formula suggestions to help you track your progress: * `=prop("Completed Tasks")/prop("Total Tasks")` to track your progress towards a goal * `=prop("Due Date")-today()` to calculate the number of days until a task is due * `=prop("Goal")=prop("Category")` to filter tasks by category

Tips and Tricks 🤫

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of this template: * Use the relation properties to link your goals and vision board items to each other * Use the tags property to add keywords to your goals and vision board items * Use the formula suggestions to create custom formulas to track your progress * Review your progress regularly and adjust your goals and vision board items as needed

By following these instructions and using this template, you will be able to set and achieve your goals, while also creating a vision board to visualize your desires. Remember to review your progress regularly and adjust your goals and vision board items as needed. Good luck! 🎉
