How to Use 📚

This Content Creation Template is designed to help you organize and streamline your content creation process. It includes several sections to help you plan, create, and track your content. To get started, simply import this template into your Notion account and begin filling out the sections.

Introduction 🌟

This template is divided into several sections, each with its own database or table. The main sections include: * Content Ideas: a database for storing and organizing your content ideas * Content Calendar: a calendar view for planning and scheduling your content * Content Creation: a database for tracking the creation and publication of your content * Analytics: a database for tracking the performance of your content

Formula Suggestions 🤔

* Use the `=count(prop("Status" = "Published"))` formula to count the number of published content pieces. * Use the `=sum(prop("Views"))` formula to calculate the total number of views for all content pieces. * Use the `=average(prop("Conversion Rate"))` formula to calculate the average conversion rate for all content pieces.

Tips and Variations 📝

* Use the `@` symbol to mention team members or collaborators in the content creation process. * Use the `#` symbol to categorize and tag your content ideas and pieces. * Use the `>` symbol to create a kanban board view for your content creation process. * Use the `<<` symbol to create a calendar view for your content schedule.
