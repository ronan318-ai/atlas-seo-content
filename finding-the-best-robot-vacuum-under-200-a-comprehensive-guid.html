Buying Guide

When shopping for the best robot vacuum under $200, there are several key features to look for:

* Suction Power: Look for a robot vacuum with a powerful suction system, ideally with a suction power of 1000Pa or higher. * Navigation System: A advanced navigation system is essential for efficient cleaning. Look for robot vacuums with infrared sensors, gyroscopes, or mapping technology. * Battery Life: A longer battery life means more cleaning time. Look for robot vacuums with a battery life of at least 60 minutes. * Dustbin Capacity: A larger dustbin capacity means less frequent emptying. Look for robot vacuums with a dustbin capacity of at least 0.5L. * Wi-Fi Connectivity: Wi-Fi connectivity allows for remote control and scheduling, making it a convenient feature for busy households. * Noise Level: A quiet operation is essential for those who plan to use their robot vacuum during the night or in noise-sensitive areas.

Red flags to avoid include:

* Low Suction Power: A suction power of less than 500Pa may not be effective for cleaning larger spaces or carpets. * Limited Navigation System: A basic navigation system may not be able to navigate through tight spaces or avoid obstacles. * Short Battery Life: A battery life of less than 30 minutes may not be sufficient for larger spaces. * Small Dustbin Capacity: A dustbin capacity of less than 0.2L may require frequent emptying.
